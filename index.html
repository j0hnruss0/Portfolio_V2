Made with Javascript (vanilla)

Click here to check it out!

Star Wars: Cantina Brawl

Made with Bootstrap CSS, Javascript and jQuery

Click here to check it out!

Replay the classic Cantina Brawl scene from Star Wars Episode 6: A New Hope with this easy-to-learn role-paying game! Pick a fighter, then battle all the other characters from the scene. Just one battle button handles all the damage and hit points. You win if you can defeat everyone else without losing all your HP!
